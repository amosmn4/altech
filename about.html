About Altech Africa

Company Overview

Altech Africa Ltd is an emerging leader in the Professional Technical Services provision market with the goal of bringing convergence and security into diverse communication systems through the use of state-of-the-art infrastructure.

Our Mission

To work with our partners and customers towards growing at the same pace with global technology while constantly being innovative to achieve satisfaction in terms of quality and integrity.

Our Vision

To be the leader in systems installations and integration including professional and technical service provision in Africa and beyond.

Core Values

  • Safety
  • Quality
  • Innovativeness
  • Efficiency and Reliability

Our History

Altech Africa was founded in 2006, with its head office in Nairobi, Kenya. The company has pursued a clear goal to fill an industry need for an expert firm covering a very broad range of services and capabilities. It operated under the business name Altech Africa until its incorporation on June 23rd, 2009, and now operates as Altech Africa Limited.

Our Team

Altech Africa Ltd is managed by a team of experts in Information and Communication Technology (ICT). We are staffed with 10 Engineers on a permanent basis and approximately 15 technicians who work with us on a contractual basis. Our diverse team brings depth and balance to each project, ranging from Data Networks, Telecommunications, Network Operating Systems, Transmission, Systems Design, and Administration.
